Who Am I?

My name is Andrew Tremblay, a Software Developer from Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada.

I enjoy developing in both enterprise software systems and web applications. As a graduate of the University of Saskatchewan in 2006 (Bachelor of Science, Honours in Computer Science, 2008), I am currently working with VendAsta Technologies in Saskatoon. I have previously worked at the University of Saskatchewan and SkipTheDishes.
